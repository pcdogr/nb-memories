Manly Beach and Promenade, c 1912

Edwardian elegance on Manly's oceanfront promenade. The beach we know today, frozen in time a century ago.

Body surfers at Manly Beach, c 1926

The birth of Australia's beach culture — body surfers riding the Manly waves, captured nearly 100 years ago.

North Steyne Promenade, Manly

Strolling along the iconic North Steyne shoreline, a favourite spot for locals and visitors alike.

Brookvale Hotel, 1955

The very first hotel in Brookvale, opened in March 1955. A landmark of the changing Northern Beaches.

Brookvale House, c 1895

Brookvale Estate captured in the late 19th century, long before the suburb we know today existed.
